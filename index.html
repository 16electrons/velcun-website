FAQ

Common questions

How does per-truck pricing work?

You pay for each active truck on the platform each month. If a truck is inactive for a full billing cycle, you're not charged. Minimum fleet sizes apply per plan.

Can I start with Dispatch and upgrade later?

Yes. Most customers start with Dispatch and add TMS, Connect, and Insights when ready. Upgrades are seamless — your data carries over instantly.

Is there a contract or setup fee?

Monthly plans are month-to-month. Annual plans save 20% and are billed yearly. Onboarding is included — no setup fees on Ecosystem and Enterprise.

Do you integrate with DAT, Truckstop, and QuickBooks?

Yes. VELCUN Connect supports major load boards, EDI partners, and accounting systems. Enterprise includes custom integration support.
